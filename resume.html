A Bit About Me

I am a seasoned UI/UX designer with over 2 years of experience in creating visually stunning and user-friendly interfaces. My expertise lies in effectively collaborating with cross-functional teams to align design solutions with user needs and business goals. I am adept at understanding feature specifications through thorough research and creative thinking, which allows me to deliver intuitive and engaging user experiences. Currently, I am focused on solving user problems for dashboard screens in the health and insurance sectors, enhancing data visualization strategies, and contributing to a compelling brand identity. As a Senior UI/UX Designer with experience at Baazi Games and Galytix Analytics, I have led the design and development of comprehensive and scalable UI components and guidelines for various applications. At Baazi Games, I enhanced the home screen UI and user experience for the CardBaazi gaming app, collaborating with teams to deeply understand user needs and issues. At Galytix Analytics, I focused on solving user problems for dashboard screens in the health and insurance sectors, enhancing data visualization and communication strategies. I consistently ensure that all designs maintain consistency, accessibility, and usability across all platforms and devices.
